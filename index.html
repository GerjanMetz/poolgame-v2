The rules!

The first person to score a ball in a pocket decides what color they play. If the first score is made by player 1, and the color is full, then player 1 plays the full colored balls, and player 2 plays half colored balls. To win, a player has to score all of his balls, and then score the 8ball (the black ball). If a player scores the 8ball before all of his balls are scored, the player loses and the other player is the winner.

Controls

To aim the cue, move the mouse left or right. Shoot with mouse1 click.
